Airbnb Rentals in Melbourne

Airbnb, Inc. is an American company that operates an online marketplace for lodging, primarily homestays for vacation rentals, and tourism activities. Based in San Francisco, California, the platform is accessible via website and mobile app. Airbnb does not own any of the listed properties; instead, it profits by receiving commission from each booking.(Airbnb,n.d.)

In this visualisation, it provides Airbnb's listings' information in Melbourne, VIC Australia. It shows the number of reviews on months of every year, prices, type of rooms, location, etc.

Airbnb Area of Listings

By looking at the map, we could see areas where it contains the number of Airbnb listings.

Ranging mostly 20-60 number of listings, we could see that the number of listings on Suburbs increases as it's closer to Melbourne City. Melbourne City iself, has 3439 number of Airbnb Listings.

Price of Top Airbnb Listings' on Suburbs

Looking at the Top 10 based on number of listings, Docklands (#5) has the highest average price of rent per bedroom, which is $112.

However, based on Top 30 number of listings, Docklands became the third highest average price of rent per bedroom, where Healesville (#23) became the first, where it has an average price of $116 per bedroom.

Review Scores based on Price and Room Type

Based on the Scatter plot, we could assume that the Review Scores of listings in Airbnb is not affected by their price. All of the room type's prices are mostly at $50-$500 range, where mostly has scores higher than 70-80.

Listings that have prices higher than $500, also have, mostly, review scores higher than 75.

Most Popular Month of Airbnb Rents

Based on the visualisations, we could see that December has the highest number of reviews overall. It could be affected by celebrations such as Christmas that is held on the month.

Other than that, March is also a popular time to travel because it is still considered peak season. However, accommodation availability and prices are usually not as tight as during the peak months of the summer.
